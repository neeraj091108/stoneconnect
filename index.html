Our Business Model

We earn only when
your trade succeeds.

No hidden fees. No monthly subscriptions to start. StoneBridge charges a transparent commission only after a trade is confirmed and payment is released from escrow.

🔍

Buyer Posts a Requirement

Buyer lists stone type, quantity, and location. Completely free — no charges at this stage.

💬

Mine Owner Sends Offer

Verified mine owners submit quotes. Buyer reviews, negotiates, and selects the best offer through the platform.

🔒

Payment Held in Escrow

Buyer deposits payment into StoneBridge's secure escrow. Mine owner ships — buyer gets peace of mind.

✅

Delivery Confirmed → Commission Released

On confirmation, funds go to the mine owner. StoneBridge deducts a 2–4% platform fee — our only revenue from the trade.
